What's in it

Two weeks. One honest report.

01

On-site observation

We spend time with the people who actually do the work — operations, procurement, production, finance. We watch how orders move, where information lives, and where it gets lost.

02

Process mapping

We map the current flow end-to-end and mark every handoff that depends on memory, spreadsheets, or chat messages. These are the points where work stalls and cost accumulates.

03

Cost quantification

We put a number on the status quo: hours lost to rework, duplicate data entry, chasing approvals, looking for files. The number is conservative and defensible.

04

Written report & proposal

You receive a written report — what we saw, what it costs, what to fix first — with a fixed-scope, fixed-price proposal attached. You decide what to do next. No retainer, no obligation.
